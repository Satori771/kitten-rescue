What We Do

Rescue

We partner with a number of local shelters who do not have the means to care for neonatal kittens, who require round-the-clock care. We also take in feral kittens off the street.

Rehabilite

Thanks to our excellent volunteers, we're able to care for the most vulnerable felines who may be just days old and have life threatening health problems, and give them all a fair chance at a full life.

Rehome

"Goodbye is the goal." That is the mantra that keeps us pushing forward. Our greatest joy is watching every one of our kittens grow strong and healthy enough to go to their forever homes.

Our Story

Satori Kitten Rescue was birthed out of one woman's dream. Our founder, Jane Doe, was working her office job when one day, her coworker called her outside. Left out in hot sun, with no food or water, were four tiny kittens in a cardboard box! She took two of them home, and quickly set to furiously googling how to bottle feed, keep kittens warm, and stimulate them to go potty.

Day by day, those two kittens grew bigger and stronger. Two months later, she officially adopted them. Their names are Molly and Digit.

Now, Jane has set out to give hundreds more kittens that same chance at life. When neonatal kittens are found abandoned, most shelters are unable to provide them with the intensive care they require. That's why fostering is such an important job! You get to be that kitten's hero!

Please Consider Supporting Us

Become a Foster

There are few jobs more rewarding than fostering kittens. The work can be hard sometimes, but seeing that tiny kitten look up at you with its milk mustache and baby blue eyes makes it all worth it. You are that little one's hero.

Donate

Our rescue is run entirely through your generous donations. By financially supporting us, you are helping us to buy much needed supplies such as kitten formula, incubators, food, medicine, and blankets.

Spread the Word!

Tell your friends about us and why they should check us out! Our mission is to save and protect the most vulnerable felines, and we want the world to know and join us!
